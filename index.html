NBA OFFSEASON NEWS 2019

Introduction

The NBA has been around for 76 years and after every season, there is a certain time known as the offseason. This is where the NBA Draft takes place, showcasing some of the greatest young talents that colleges and around the world have to offer. Then, there is the Free Agency period, where teams can sign players that are out of their contracts. Also, teams can make trades with other teams if they want a certain player who is under a contract. Over the years, the offseason has gotten more interesting and this year, it seems to be that we have witnessed possibly the greatest NBA summer ever. A whole frenzy of events have taken place and those are what are going to be recapped today.

Down below are the topics that will be re-visited and analyzed. These are all the of the main events that took place this summer and what is going to be coming up next season. Everything you need to know about the summer and next season are all right here, as you can catch up on probably the most popular sport in North America, basketball in its highest form, the NBA.
