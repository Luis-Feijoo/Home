8 Teams Who Could Win A Championship 2019-20

8. Boston Celtics

Starting 5 -- PG - Kemba Walker SG - Jaylen Brown SF - Gordon Hayward PF - Jayson Tatum C - Enes Kanter

Images: Google Images

The Celtics have underachieved under Brad Stevens and a team of great young players. Even with the acquisition of Kyrie Irving in 2017, the Celtics still haven't been able to make the Finals and have dissapointed in terms of expectations. They did get Kemba Walker but the Celtics do lack size and a defensive presence outside of Marcus Smart and Enes Kanter. Unfortunately, due to the Celtics' past efforts, they will probably not be able to get past the 2nd round.

7. Toronto Raptors

Starting 5 -- PG - Kyle Lowry SG - Norman Powell SF - Pascal Siakam PF - Serge Ibaka C - Marc Gasol

Images: Google Images

The Raptors just won the NBA Championship last June with Kawhi Leonard, Kyle Lowry and others helped the franchise win their first ring in 24 years. The Raptors eventually did lose Kawhi Leonard in free agency, but they have Lowry and Siakam, two solid all-star caliber players. They also have a great bench led by Fred VanVleet, who balled out in the Finals. Although, the Raptors ultimately lack a top scorer and defensive beast, which could cost them in the playoffs.

6. Philadelphia 76ers

Starting 5 -- PG - Ben Simmons SG - Josh Richardson SF - Tobias Harris PF - Al Horford C - Joel Embiid

Image: Google Images

The Sixers are a very good team that just lack the playoff experience because they have only made the playoffs twice during the last 7 years. Although with Ben Simmons at the point and Embiid being the beast he is, the Sixers have a real chance this year as the East has weakened even more. The Raptors have lost their star player and the Celtics also lost Kyrie. They have a real chance as long as the injury-prone Embiid doesn't catch a stop and the Sixers can mash their renewed team together in time for May.

5. Milwaukee Bucks

Starting 5 -- PG - Eric Bledsoe SG - Wesley Matthews SF - Khris Middleton PF - Giannis Antetokounmpo C - Brook Lopez

Image: Google Images

The Bucks were statistically the best team in the East last season. Although, they lacked the playoff experience that teams such as the Raptors had. They have a tall lineup with big men capable of shooting and going in the post. Even with the loss of Malcolm Brogdon, the Bucks still have a brilliant team led under a great coach known for great offensive play. They have the MVP Giannis and they have a young core that can help him out vastly in the playoffs; as they should be capable of making the ECF this season.

4. Golden State Warriors

Starting 5 -- PG - Steph Curry SG - D'Angelo Russell SF - Glenn Robinson III PF - Draymond Green C - Willie Cauley-Stein

Image: Google Images

The Golden State Warriors have built a dynasty surrounding Steph Curry along with Klay Thompson and Draymond Green. They had brought Kevin Durant, a top player which eventually resulted in 2 rings. Now that Durant's gone, they have to fill the scoring void. So they got D'Angelo Russell, a young promising PG with scoring abilities that is capable of filling in that void. Unfortunately, the Warriors have to play without Klay Thompson and fans are not too sure that D-Lo and Curry will mesh well in their first season. It is only a matter of hope and determination that can help the "Dubs" make it far in the playoffs.

3. Houston Rockets

Starting 5 -- PG - Russell Westbrook SG - James Harden SF - Eric Gordon PF - P.J. Tucker C - Clint Capela

Images: Google Images

The Houston Rockets have always been lead to the playoffs with James Harden as the cornerstone of the franchise. Although, he has not been able to lead them to a championship either with Dwight Howard or Chris Paul. He gets another chance though, as he now teams up once again with 2017 MVP Russell Westbrook as his sidekick. They look to lead the Rockets to the NBA Finals, as they have not been able to reach that point since '95. They have a solid line-up filled with big men and shooters. The problem is that the chemistry between Harden and Westbrook might not flow very well, which could lead to ego issues in the locker room and on the court.

2. LA Lakers

Starting 5 -- PG - Rajon Rondo SG - Danny Green SF - LeBron James PF - Anthony Davis C - Dwight Howard

Images: Google Images

The Los Angeles Lakers are probably the most popular team in not just basketball or the U.S., but all over the world. They have not been able to replicate the success that Kobe, Shaq, and more helped bring to L.A. over this decade. But with LeBron and now AD after the trade, the Lakers seem to be top contenders once again. They have great depth, strong shooting, and 2 of the game's best players teaming up for nasty pick and rolls. They look to make the playoffs once again since 2013 and it seems possible with LeBron and AD leading the team, as they look to make it to the finals. They seem to also be a lock for the WCF as well.

1. LA Clippers

Starting 5 -- PG - Patrick Beverley SG - Landry Shamet SF - Kawhi Leonard PF - Paul George C - Ivica Zubac

Images: Google Images

The Los Angeles Clippers were already a good team before they got two of the best small forwards in the game. They had taken the Golden State Warriors to 6 games in last season's playoffs with no all-stars. They now boast one of the best teams in the West with Kawhi Leonard and Paul George, and they have one of the best defensive teams in the league! The 2 MVP candidates should be able to lead a solid year for the Clippers, but as long as they don't get any injuries, considering the history; they should be an easy lock for the Conference Finals.
